# Landing Page

## Purpose

Introduce MXBLOR to prospective and returning users, explain its core building-management capabilities, and provide direct paths to sign in or contact the service provider.

## Layout and content

- A branded hero presents the MXBLOR name, the “Easy. Quick. Transparent.” promise, a concise product description, and a visual portfolio preview.
- The primary action opens sign-in for anonymous visitors and the dashboard for authenticated visitors.
- A secondary action starts an email to request a demonstration.
- Two responsive feature groups describe common expenses, users and roles, financial reporting, auditing, web/mobile use, portfolio insight, language support, and tenant-aware security.
- A pricing and onboarding section explains that plans are tailored to the managed portfolio.
- The footer links directly to email, telephone, the M. Halloumas website, and the supported social profiles.

## States and behavior

- The page is public and remains available to authenticated users.
- Content follows the active English or Greek application language.
- All sections adapt from a single-column mobile layout to a wider desktop layout.
- Contact actions open the appropriate external email, telephone, website, or social destination; the page does not submit a contact form.
